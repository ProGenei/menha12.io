About Menha
It is a platform that provides college scholarships to outstanding students through donations from community members.
Goals
Cooperating with private universities in Saudi Arabia formally in studying the student’s case studies to examine the extent to which they fulfill the required conditions, after which the student’s level, acceptance letter and the amount of money he needs on the site are presented. This letter will be renewed annually so that we can display many cases on the site
